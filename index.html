About me

Call me Khoa (a subword of Nguyễn Đình Đăng Khoa in Vietnamese). I am an incomming PhD student at University of Virginia.

I completed bachelor degree in University of Science - VNU. During my undergraduate studies, I had several research experiences:

  • I wrote my thesis under the supervision of Dr. Tuan Anh Bui on the cohomology of SSS-arithmetic groups.
  • I joined Vietnam Polymath REU program with the topic “LOT groups and the Whitehead Conjecture”, mentored by Prof. Thomas Koberda.
  • I worked with Prof. Tuan Hiep Dang on Reiner-Tudose Conjecture.
  • I worked with Prof. Truong Le Hoang on K-stability condition of Fano variety, funded by VINIF within sponsorship and cooperation program in 2024 between Vietnam Institute of Mathematics and VINIF.

I do coding for fun.
